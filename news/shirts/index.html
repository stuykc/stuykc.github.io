2016 KC Apparel Design Contest

Due: January 21st, 2017

Late submissions won’t be accepted (maybe).

GuideLines:

  • The design can be anything as long as it has "Stuyvesant Key Club" or some variation of it such as “StuyKC.” You can contact us if you want to make sure your variation is acceptable.
  • Email us a PNG of your design as well as share a Customink link so we can see where your design goes and the color of the hoodie.
  • The winning design will be chosen by a club-wide vote sometime in early February.

Ideas:

  • Mascot: Panda and Beaver
  • KC Colors: White, Blue, and Gold
  • Motto: Caring— Our way of life
