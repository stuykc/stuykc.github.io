--- layout: page title: About Stuyvesant Key Club --- Stuyvesant Key Club was founded many years ago by a group of dedicated students willing to help and volunteer in their community. Starting from a small club at Stuy, StuyKC has grown to a club consisting of over 200 students.

Our Pledge!

I pledge, on my honor, to uphold the Objects of Key Club International; to build my home, school and community; to serve my nation and God; and combat all forces which tend to undermine these institutions.

Our Motto!

Caring - Our Way of Life
